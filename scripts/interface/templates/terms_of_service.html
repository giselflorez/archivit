TERMS OF SERVICE

Please review and accept to continue using ARCHIV-IT

1. Purpose & Mission

ARCHIV-IT is designed to help artists and collectors preserve, understand, and visualize their creative history. This tool exists to protect and empower the digital art community.

2. Acceptable Use

You may use ARCHIV-IT to:

  • Archive and visualize your own creative work and collection history
  • Understand relationships between your work, collectors, and the broader ecosystem
  • Export and preserve your artistic legacy
  • Build context layers from your own social and blockchain data

3. Prohibited Activities

The following activities are strictly prohibited and will result in immediate license revocation:

  • Art Theft & Copycatting: Using this tool to identify, copy, or re-mint artwork created by others
  • Impersonation: Creating archives that misrepresent your identity or claim others' work as your own
  • Data Harvesting: Using scraped data to target, harass, or exploit other artists or collectors
  • Fraudulent Provenance: Fabricating or manipulating provenance data
  • Circumvention: Attempting to disable, bypass, or remove protection mechanisms

4. Data Consistency & Trust Indicator

ARCHIV-IT includes a Trust Indicator - a visual orb that shows how well your imported data aligns across sources:

  • A full, green-glowing orb = excellent data consistency across blockchain and social sources
  • A partially filled orb = moderate consistency, some gaps to address
  • An empty, red-glowing orb = low consistency - significant gaps between claimed history and verifiable data

This indicator is visible to you and reflects how your profile may appear to others using ARCHIV-IT. It's not a judgment - it's a mirror. Others in the ecosystem can see this indicator when viewing aggregated data, and will naturally draw their own conclusions.

A red orb speaks for itself.

5. License Revocation & Appeals

If your license is revoked for violation of these terms:

  • You will receive notification with the reason
  • You have 30 days to submit an appeal
  • Appeals are reviewed by WEB3GISEL within 14 days
  • You may export your data during the appeal period
  • Final decisions are at the sole discretion of WEB3GISEL

6. Local-First Privacy

Your data stays on your local system. ARCHIV-IT does not transmit your archive data to external servers. The only exceptions:

  • Anonymous usage analytics (if opted in)
  • Appeal communications you initiate

7. Updates to Terms

These terms may be updated. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance. Material changes will be highlighted at next launch.
