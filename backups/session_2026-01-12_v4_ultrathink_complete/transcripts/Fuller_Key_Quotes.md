## VERIFIED KEY QUOTES

### On Change (Mike Vance Interview, pre-1983)
> **FULLER:** "You never change anything by fighting it; you change things by making them obsolete through superior technology."

*Source: Mike Vance, "Think Out of the Box," p.138*

### On Spaceship Earth (Operating Manual for Spaceship Earth, 1969)
> **FULLER:** "Now there is one outstandingly important fact regarding Spaceship Earth, and that is that no instruction book came with it."

> **FULLER:** "We are all astronauts on a little spaceship called Earth."

*Source: Operating Manual for Spaceship Earth (1969)*

### "Trimtab" Philosophy
> **FULLER:** "Call me Trimtab."

*Explanation: A trimtab is the small rudder that moves the big rudder of a ship. Fuller saw himself as making small changes that cause big shifts. This is on his gravestone.*

*Source: Playboy Interview, February 1972*

### On Human Purpose (Playboy 1972)
> **FULLER:** "The most important thing to teach your children is that the sun does not rise and set. It is the Earth that revolves around the sun. Then teach them the concepts of North, South, East and West, and that they relate to where they happen to be on the planet's surface at that time. Everything else will follow."

*Source: Playboy Interview, February 1972*

### On Wealth
> **FULLER:** "Wealth is our organized capability to cope effectively with the environment in sustaining our healthy regeneration and decreasing both the physical and metaphysical restrictions of the forward days of our lives."

*Source: Critical Path (1981)*

### On Integrity
> **FULLER:** "Integrity is the essence of everything successful."

*Source: Various lectures*

## "EVERYTHING I KNOW" (1975)

42-hour lecture series delivered in January 1975 in Philadelphia.
Fuller, at 79 years old, attempts to convey everything he has learned.

**Key Themes:**
1. Synergetics and geometry
2. Comprehensive anticipatory design science
3. "Doing more with less"
4. Ephemeralization
5. World Game
6. Dymaxion principles

**Transcripts available:** [BFI.org](https://bfi.org/about-fuller/everything-i-know/)

## COGNITIVE MAPPING NOTES

**Expression Style:**
- Invented new vocabulary ("synergy," "tensegrity," "ephemeralization")
- Long, flowing sentences
- Systems thinking made verbal
- Encyclopedic recall

**Physical Presence:**
- Diminutive stature
- Thick glasses
- Animated gestures
- Could lecture for hours without notes

**Key Characteristics for NORTHSTAR:**
- Comprehensive thinking (everything connected)
- Optimism through technology
- "Doing more with less" as mantra
- Individual as agent of change (trimtab)
- Geometry as fundamental language
