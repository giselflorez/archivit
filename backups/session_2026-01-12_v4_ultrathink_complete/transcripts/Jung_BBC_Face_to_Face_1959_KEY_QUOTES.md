## VERIFIED KEY QUOTES

### On God and Belief
> **JUNG:** "I don't believe, I know."

*Context: When asked if he believes in God. This quote sparked considerable public debate at the time.*

### On Meaning
> **JUNG:** "Man cannot stand a meaningless life."

*Context: Discussion about psychological wellbeing and purpose.*

### On Self-Awareness
> **JUNG:** "I am what I am."

*Context: Describing his moment of self-realization at age eleven.*

### On Death
> **JUNG:** (Advised elderly patients) "to live as if they had centuries ahead of them."

*Context: Discussion about approaching mortality with openness.*

### On the Psyche
> **JUNG:** (Argued) "the psyche may not be confined to space and time."

*Context: Discussion of consciousness and its nature.*

## INTERVIEW TOPICS COVERED

1. **Early Life & Childhood**
   - Parents' influence
   - Moment of self-awareness at age 11
   - Early religious experiences

2. **Career Path**
   - Originally wanted archaeology
   - Financial constraints led to medicine
   - Psychiatry textbook inspired specialization

3. **Relationship with Freud**
   - Met in 1907
   - Analyzed each other's dreams
   - Fundamental disagreements
   - "Purely personal approach" critique

4. **Clinical Work**
   - Schizophrenic patient with sun symbolism
   - Connection to ancient Mithraic texts
   - Development of psychological types

5. **Philosophy of Life**
   - Meaning and purpose
   - Death and mortality
   - Religious experience vs. belief
