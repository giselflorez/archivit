# Finding Aligned Engineers & Artists

## Signals of Alignment

People who talk about:
- **Local-first** software
- **User sovereignty** / data ownership
- **Anti-extraction** / anti-surveillance
- **Human-AI collaboration** (not replacement)
- **Open source** as principle, not marketing
- **Decentralization** as architecture, not buzzword

---

## Where to Find Them

### Web3 / Crypto Art
- **Async Art** community (collaborative, on-chain)
- **Art Blocks** artists (generative, code-native)
- **Tezos art community** (lower fees = more accessible)
- **fxhash** creators
- **Zora** / **Mirror** writers

### AI Ethics / Sovereignty
- **LocalAI** community
- **Ollama** users (local LLMs)
- **Mozilla AI** initiatives
- **EFF** (Electronic Frontier Foundation) aligned
- **Solid Project** (Tim Berners-Lee's data pods)

## Red Flags (NOT Aligned)

- "Web3" but everything is on AWS
- "Decentralized" but requires their token
- "AI-powered" but scrapes without consent
- "Open" but funded by surveillance capital
- Talks sovereignty, builds walled gardens

---

## Outreach Approach

Don't pitch. Show the work.

1. Share the verification engine concept
2. Share the collaboration philosophy
3. Ask: "Does this align with what you're building?"
4. Let them self-select

The right people will recognize it.
