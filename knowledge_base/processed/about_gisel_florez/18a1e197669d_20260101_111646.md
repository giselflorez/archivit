## Content
**Gisel Florez** is a New York City-based artist and RISD alumna ('03) specializing in manual camera and video techniques with a conceptual focus on waves, light, time, and human interconnectedness.[1][2][3][5]

She has over 17 years of experience in photographic arts, including high-end luxury and lifestyle product photography for advertising and editorials, and maintains a home/studio in Brooklyn, NY.[1][2][5] Her exhibitions include NFT.NYC, Winkleman Gallery, NYICG WallSpace, Museum of Crypto Art, and "She Art" VR in Cryptovoxels; she co-founded the WOCA (Women of Crypto Art) collective and has presented at events like "Bridge to the Metaverse" with Artory and MOCDA.[1] In 2021, Fortune Magazine ranked her #36 among top NFT influencers, with additional features in The New York Times, Vogue, WhiteHotMagazine podcast, "Behind the Art" with Josie Bellini, Curating Crypto Podcast, and CryptoArtWeek 2020.[4][1] Her work is available for viewing and purchase on platforms like Saatchi Art (24 artworks) and is open to special projects and commissions.[2][1]
