## Content
**Gisel Florez** is a **photographic and digital artist** who works extensively in blockchain and crypto art, exploring interconnectedness and the nature of existence through manual camera techniques and conceptual practice.[1][5]

Based in NYC with over 17 years of experience in photographic arts, Florez has become a prominent figure in the crypto art movement.[1] Her work focuses on **visual studies of waves and light**, examining how we are interconnected within the space of existence.[1][5] She employs traditional still and cinematic methods combined with manual camera and video techniques to create her conceptual pieces.[1][6]

Florez's contributions to blockchain art include:

- **Co-founder of Women of Crypto Art (WoCA)**, a collective supporting women artists in the crypto space[1][2]
- **Featured artist** in "CRYPTO ART - Begins," the first NFT book documenting the evolution of crypto art[3]
- **Exhibitions and presentations** at major venues including NFTNYC, Winkleman Gallery, Museum of Crypto Art, and "She Art" VR exhibition in Cryptovoxels[1]
- **NFT acquisitions and patronage**, including winning the Genesis M○C△ ROOM NFT in a bidding war on the Museum of Crypto Art platform[2]
- **Virtual world presence** with spaces in Cryptovoxels, Decentraland, and Sandbox[2]

Florez is also a RISD alumni and photographic affinity leader who remains open to special projects and commissions.[1]
