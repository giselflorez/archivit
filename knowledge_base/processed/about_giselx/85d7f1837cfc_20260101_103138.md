## Content
**GiselX (Gisel X Florez or GISELXFlorez)** is a visual artist specializing in manual 4x5 photography and cinematic techniques, blending traditional methods with digital and Web3 expressions to explore conceptual themes.[1][2][4][5][6]

She describes herself as an "Artist in Light," using photographic and cinematic mediums in a post-digital era, with over 20 years of expertise in photographic arts, visual direction, and emergent technologies.[2][4][5][6] Her work appears on NFT platforms like Foundation, Rarible (e.g., "420 SPECIAL EDITION" series split into 6 portions), SuperRare (giselxvault with 47 owned pieces), and Cent.[2][3][6][7]

- Personal sites: giselflorez.com, giselx.art, web3photo.com (focuses on visual media consulting for brands and creatives).[2][4][5]
