## Content
**GiselX (also known as Gisel Florez or GISELXTEZ) is a New York-based artist specializing in manual 4x5 photographic and cinematic mediums expressed digitally, often exploring themes of light, waves, and interconnectedness.** She describes herself as an "Artist in Light," blending conceptual practices with photography, video, and Web3 technologies like NFTs.[1][3][5][6]
