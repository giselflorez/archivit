## THE PROBLEM

You already know it.

Every photo you post, every click you make, every second you spend online - someone else profits from it. The platforms you use daily have built empires on your attention, your creativity, your data.

They call you a "user." But you're not using them. They're using you.

- Your data lives on their servers
- Your content trains their AI
- Your attention is sold to advertisers
- Your creativity builds their wealth
- Your identity exists at their permission

And when you want to leave? Good luck taking your digital life with you.

---

## THE VISION

What if it didn't have to be this way?

What if there was software that:
- **Ran on your computer** (not their cloud)
- **Kept your data local** (not uploaded anywhere)
- **Served your goals** (not engagement metrics)
- **Let you leave anytime** (with everything you own)
- **Never showed you ads** (because you're not the product)

What if the tools for creating, organizing, and sharing your work actually belonged to you?

*"Your Data. Your Archive. Your Foundation."*

- Never lose another file
- **Your archive. Your rules.**

### IT-R8: Your Studio
*"Create from Your Data"*

Turn your archive into new creations. No coding required. Just creativity.

- Generate designs from your own work

### SOCI-8: Your Network
*"Share Without Surrendering"*

- Share what you choose

## WHAT MAKES THIS DIFFERENT

### It's Local-First
Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it. No cloud dependency. No server you don't control. No "terms of service" that give companies rights to your work.

### It's Truly Free
The free tier isn't free because we're selling your data. It's free because we believe everyone deserves these tools. No ads. No tracking. No catch.

### It's Yours Forever
Export everything. Delete everything. Leave anytime. Your data is portable and yours. We'll never hold your digital life hostage.

### It Works Offline
No internet? No problem. Your tools still work. Your data is still there. Because it's on YOUR device.
