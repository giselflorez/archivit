## GLOBAL UNDERSTANDING

### Biography

Robert Edward Grant is a polymath, serial entrepreneur, prolific inventor, and thought leader. With over 80 patents to his name, Grant bridges science, spirituality, and innovation. He holds a BA from Brigham Young University and an MBA from Thunderbird School of Global Management.

Grant served as CEO and President of Bausch and Lomb Surgical before founding Strathspey Crown LLC, a growth equity holding company spanning healthcare, clean energy, social media, and financial technology. He is also Founder, Chairman, and CEO of Crown Sterling Limited LLC, a cryptography company based on his mathematical discoveries.

His work spans mathematics, physics, music theory, sacred geometry, and consciousness studies, seeking to unify these disciplines through the language of number and form.

### NORTHSTAR

**THE GEOMETRIC UNIFICATION OF ALL KNOWLEDGE** - Grant's NORTHSTAR is the revelation that mathematics is a sacred language connecting all disciplines - physics, music, biology, consciousness - through geometric principles. His mission is demonstrating that prime numbers, fundamental constants, and sacred geometries are not separate phenomena but expressions of one underlying divine pattern.

### Core Belief

> "Math isn't just numbers - it's a sacred language, a bridge to higher consciousness."

Grant believes that the universe operates according to beautiful mathematical principles that, once understood, reveal the unity of all knowledge and the divine nature of reality.

### Specific Techniques

**Quasi-Prime Analysis:**
Grant's methodology identifies prime numbers through their geometric position when integers are distributed around polygons with sides as multiples of 6:
1. Distribute integers around icositetragon (24-sided polygon)
2. Prime numbers occupy specific geometric moduli
3. Non-prime numbers sharing these moduli ("quasi-primes") reveal unique properties
4. Digital root analysis identifies primes and prime factors without trial division

**Icositetragon Wheel:**
- 24-position circular arrangement of ascending integers
- Each 360-degree turn advances through number spiral
- Prime-relevant patterns emerge at specific positions
- Forms basis for prime prediction methodology

**Precise Temperament Tuning:**
- Base frequency: 432.081 Hz (vs standard 440 Hz)
- Aligns musical intervals (major third, perfect fifth) with sacred geometric ratios

- Connects sound frequency to consciousness and well-being
